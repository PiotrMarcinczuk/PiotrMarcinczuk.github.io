Calculator

My calculator has a two way to use math operations. First way is a operation without equal sign If we click in sequence 5, +, 6 and another math sign like - or *, we get a result previous operations (11- from previous sequence)(more specifically on github).
Second way is operation with '=' sign. If we click in sequence 1, +, 3, and now click = we get result of operations below components with which result has create (more specifically on github).
